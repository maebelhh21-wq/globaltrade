What is GlobalTrade Manager?

GlobalTrade Manager is a comprehensive web-based platform designed to help import/export companies streamline operations, reduce paperwork, and improve productivity. Whether you're managing international shipments, tracking inventory, analyzing performance, or organizing daily tasks — we’ve got you covered.

Key Functions

  • 📄 Document Management — Upload, download, and auto-generate trade documents like Commercial Invoice, Bill of Lading, and Certificate of Origin.
  • 🛍️ Store & Inventory — Manage your product catalog, SKUs, stock levels, and pricing in real time.
  • 📊 Performance Reports — View and export weekly, monthly, and yearly reports with visual charts and KPIs.
  • ✅ Task & Goal Tracker — Set daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly goals and track completion to stay on target.
